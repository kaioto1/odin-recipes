Banana raspberry almond smoothie recipe

Description

A creamy, refreshing smoothie with the natural sweetness of banana, the tart brightness of raspberries, and the nutty richness of almond butter. Great for breakfast, post-workout, or a clean snack.

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe banana
  • ¾ cup frozen raspberries
  • 1 tbsp almond butter
  • 1 cup almond milk (or any milk of choice)
  • 1 tsp honey or maple syrup (optional)
  • A few ice cubes (optional, for thicker texture)

Steps

  1. Add ingredients to a blender: banana, raspberries, almond butter, and almond milk.
  2. Optional sweetening: Add honey or maple syrup if desired.
  3. Blend on high for 30–60 seconds, until smooth.
  4. Check texture: Add more milk for thinner, or ice cubes for thicker.
  5. Serve immediately in a chilled glass.
